# AI Gate Iraq — Pilot Demo Script

This script acts as a guide for presenters, technical leads, and stakeholders showcasing the **AI Gate Iraq** platform to clients, target traders, and pilot project groups.

---

## ⏱️ Demo Option 1: The 3-Minute Quick Launch

*Focus: Fast, visual engagement and high-impact features.*

1. **Minute 1: The Persona & The Entry (Onboarding)**
   - Start with the workspace open. Mention: *"Importing goods in Iraq currently requires endless phone calls, unpredictable costs, and legal guesswork."*
   - Briefly point out the **Onboarding Guide** or **Pilot Honesty Banner** to establish credibility and clear, bounded expectations.
2. **Minute 2: Interactive AI Advisory & Quick Calculations**
   - Click the **AI Business Advisor** chip or type a quick query. Point out how the AI seamlessly answers complex Iraqi customs regulations in English, Kurdish, or Arabic.
   - Jump immediately to the **Cost Estimator** or **Currency Converter**. Type a value (e.g., $10,000 USD to parallel IQD) and generate a mock cost breakdown on the spot.
3. **Minute 3: Logistics Visibility & Close**
   - Click **Border Status** or **Shipment Tracking** to show visual wait-time indicators and operational checkpoints.
   - Close on the core takeaway: *"This unified cockpit turns fragmented trade friction into a clean digital gateway, saving countless hours for Iraqi SMEs."*

## ⏱️ Demo Option 2: The 5-Minute Controlled Deep Dive

*Focus: Full flow showing intelligence pairing with practical utility engines.*

### 1. Welcome & Onboarding (0:00 - 0:45)
- **Action**: Open the app or trigger the Onboarding Guide.
- **Presenter Script**:
  > *"Welcome to AI Gate Iraq. Our digital platform is tailored specifically for traders, SMEs, and logistics drivers navigating the complex Iraqi logistics landscape. By integrating an advanced conversational AI with precise trade tools, we bypass typical operational guesswork."*
- **Visuals**: Show onboarding steps, pilot limitations text, and user group selection.

### 2. Conversational Advisor & Context Aware Scope (0:45 - 2:00)
- **Action**: Select the **AI Business Advisor** tab. Ask a standard pilot query (e.g., *"How do I import food commodities through Ibrahim Khalil?"*).
- **Presenter Script**:
  > *"Our AI Business Advisor has targeted knowledge of Iraq's primary trade corridors, custom declarations, and regional administrative structures. If I ask a complex importing question, it replies instantly with structured, clear instructions in our preferred language."*
- **Visuals**: Show streaming, styled markdown chat response.

### 3. Practical Calculator & Estimates (2:00 - 3:15)
- **Action**: Switch to the **Currency Converter** and convert an import batch. Then, jump to the **Cost Estimator** and estimate a cargo ship load of construction materials.
- **Presenter Script**:
  > *"Once our trader understands the policy, they need to run immediate calculations. Here, we can instantly compare the official Central Bank currency rate with the parallel market rate. From there, the Cost Estimator calculates estimated tariffs, handling, and logistics fees based on weight and volume."*
- **Visuals**: Input variables into the form, see instantly calculated summary sheets.

### 4. Operational Readiness: Border & Procurement (3:15 - 4:30)
- **Action**: Click **Border Status** and review wait times. Then go to **Procurement & Sourcing** and generate a RFQ template.
- **Presenter Script**:
  > *"Before dispatching vehicles, operators check the Border Status dashboard to assess real-time wait trends. In procurement, a trader can quickly draft a structured sourcing request, formatted perfectly to share with global supply partner vendors."*
- **Visuals**: Show responsive, styled cards, status badges, and copying of generated procurement text.

### 5. Supply Chain Tracking & Wrap (4:30 - 5:00)
- **Action**: Put a sample tracking ID inside the **Shipment Tracking** text field. View the dynamic progress stepper.
- **Presenter Script**:
  > *"Finally, traders track active cargo within the Shipment Tracker, monitoring milestone customs releases and border clearance steps. While this represents a high-fidelity pilot prototype, it lays the secure foundations of what a unified Iraqi digital corridor looks like."*

## 💡 Crucial Guide: Speaking about Pilot Limitations

When showcasing **AI Gate Iraq**, maintaining absolute honesty builds immense trust. Be proactive about describing the project boundaries.

### What NOT to Claim ❌
- **Do NOT claim live automated connections to restricted databases or protected third-party systems** (e.g., *"This is directly pulling live databases from border offices"*).
- **Do NOT claim financial certification** (e.g., *"This is an official exchange platform approved by the Central Bank of Iraq"*).
- **Do NOT claim real-time GPS tracking of active commercial vehicles in the wild**.

### How to Phrase It Beautifully ✅
- **On Data Sources**: *"Our platforms utilize high-fidelity operational logic and baseline parameters to demonstrate complete visual capabilities. In the fully scaled production version, this module adapts secure APIs directly from regional port authorities."*
- **On Rules & Regulations**: *"The conversational advisor structures complex guidelines into intuitive formats. For heavy industrial shipping, we advise users to cross-reference recommendations with licensed regional trade brokers."*
- **On Purpose**: *"This is an active pilot demonstration workspace designed to prove the incredible efficiency of consolidating Kurdish, Arabic, and English workflows for modern cross-border operations."*
